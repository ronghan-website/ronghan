About Me

I am a dedicated mathematics researcher with a passion for theoretical analysis and practical applications. My work focuses on the intersection of pure mathematics and computational methods, with particular emphasis on partial differential equations and their numerical solutions.

With expertise in functional analysis, numerical methods, and scientific computing, I aim to contribute to both the mathematical community and real-world problem solving through rigorous research and mentoring.

Research Interests
